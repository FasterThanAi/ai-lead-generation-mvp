FAQ

Q: What content can be uploaded?
A: Teams can start with onboarding documents, SOPs, internal training notes, policy documents, and role-specific learning material.

Q: What does the system create?
A: The system creates short training videos, quizzes, doubt support, and HR analytics from the provided material.

Q: Is a pilot recommended?
A: Yes. A pilot with one department is recommended before a larger rollout.

Q: Does pricing depend on requirements?
A: Yes. Pricing depends on employees, training modules, analytics needs, and support requirements.
